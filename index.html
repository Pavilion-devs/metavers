Blockchain Technology

Blockchains are distributed and encrypted databases. It allows data stored and transferred securely so that no one except the data owner can tamper with it.

Furthermore, many people consider them an essential part of the metaverse concept. In healthcare, their most obvious use case is managing and securing our precious health data.
